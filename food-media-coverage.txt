SECTION 6: FOOD FOR THOUGHT FEST - KEY FACTS
================================================================================

ORGANIZER: 
South Asian Association for Gastronomy (SAAG) — founded and run by Maneesh & 
Sonali Baheti (Founders of Haute Services)

FOUNDED: 
2015 (Inaugural edition at India Habitat Centre, New Delhi)

FORMAT:
Multi-day festival with three core zones:
- Food Fest: Curated food court and pop-ups representing South Asian countries
- Thought Fest: Panels, keynote talks, and interviews with chefs, writers, 
  policy-makers, and cultural figures
- Fun Fest: Evening entertainment, cultural performances, and family-friendly 
  experiences

MISSION:
To be the authoritative South-Asia gastronomy platform: a networking hub for 
food professionals, a knowledge consolidator for culinary traditions, and a 
thought leadership forum where food culture, policy and innovation meet. The 
festival stresses "regional unity through culinary diversity."

KEY ACHIEVEMENTS:
- Only South Asian festival featured FOUR TIMES by Associated Press (A.P.)
- Recognized as "Food Festival of the Year 2019" - National Gastronomy Recognition
- Gastronomy Event of the Year 2025 - Peaklife Gourmet Awards
- 7+ Editions across India
- 50+ Master Chefs participated
- Featured across leading national television networks

NOTABLE LOCATIONS:
- 2015: India Habitat Centre, New Delhi (Inaugural)
- 2016-2019: Delhi venues (Taj Palace, GMR Aerocity)
- 2019: GMR Square, Aerocity, New Delhi (Dec 14-15)
- 2022: Delhi-area programming
- 2024-2025: Sabarmati Riverfront Event Centre, Ahmedabad (with Ahmedabad 
  Municipal Corporation)
